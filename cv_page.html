Short resume


After completing my engineering studies in Applied Mathematics (Data Science specialization) at CY Tech in Cergy (France) and the Master 2 MODO (Modelling, Optimization, Decision and Organization) in Paris-Dauphine University (PSL), I am currently enrolled in the Master 2 Probability and Finance (ex DEA El Karoui) at Ecole Polytechnique.

In summer 2023, I performed a 3 month research internship at CoDAlab in the Polytechnic University from Catalonia, where I used machine learning methods, especially domain adaptation methods and binary classifiers, to detect faults in wind turbines. I had the chance to publish an article about it with my supervisors at the 2024 IEEE I2MTC conference.

I have just finished a 6 month internship at Bpifrance as a Quant Engineer where I have worked on the joint modelling of interest rates and the default rate of an insurance product.

I worked on different projects during the past years. You can see an overview of it in the projects section.
